A Christian View of Rhetoric

This essay was my first introduction to the history of rhetoric. Even though rhetoric is not one of my favorite subjects, I learned that it has a varied and interesting history and development into what it is today. I think one of the major obstacles I faced while writing this essay was distilling the vast amount of information into a shorter readable essay. Usually I have to research more information in order to reach a certain length, but this essay required me to decide what information to include and what to leave out. This was a new and interesting challenge that presented itself again while preparing to give a speech based on this paper. I needed to find a balance between keeping the speech short and still presenting some depth of information.
