About Me

My name is Elijah Goossen and I am studying Electrical Engineering at Dordt College, soon to be Dordt University.

I grew up in Nairobi, Kenya, as a missionary kid. When I was nine years old, we moved back to the states and I started attending Omaha Christian Academy in Omaha, NE. I love to read science fiction and fantasy and enjoy watching sports with my friends.

I found out about Dordt through some families at school who had children that attended Dordt. When I visited, I really liked the feel of the campus community and the high priority that the faculty and staff put on their faith. I have been challenged in my faith while attending Dordt, especially in reconciling different perspectives about various theological topics, but I think that God will use this challenge to strengthen my faith and help me trust in Him.
